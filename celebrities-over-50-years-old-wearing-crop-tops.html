Celebrities Over 50 Years Old Wearing Crop Tops

March 30, 2024
blog

CREAM OF THE CROP

You may only associate crop tops with supermodel street style outfits or music festival must-haves, but these stars prove they can look tasteful and appropriate for any occasion, at any age! See how these celebs are giving us outfit (and workout!) inspo with their midriff-baring looks.

HALLE BERRY, 50

She wore a gown with a completely see-through bodice to the Academy Awards (you remember it!), and we're happy to see Halle's "sheer" commitment to the look continues with this bra-exposed number.

VIOLA DAVIS, 51

We've seen a lot of interesting outfits at the airport. So for us to be impressed by Viola's sheer crop top, sleek blazer and leather pants to impress us, you know it's a memorable outfit.

MARISA TOMEI, 52

Marisa's cutout may be mini, but it does a mighty job of showing off her insane body.

ELLE MACPHERSON, 53

Elle's tropical vacay is further proof she's found the fountain of youth - who else can say their loungewear is just as sizzling as their itty-bitty bikinis?

DEMI MOORE, 54

Demi's daughter, Tallulah, also couldn't believe how ageless her mother looked in her sparkly crop top and matching pleated skirt writing on Instagram, "Mom is literal teenager."

FELICITY HUFFMAN, 54

A flared pair of fuchsia Ripley Rader pants is ambitious to any of us, but Felicity's tiny top created the perfect proportions (while also showing off her killer bod!).

KATHY GRIFFIN, 56

A crop top plus a number of sexy cutouts equals Kathy killing it both onstage and off.

ANGELA BASSETT, 58

The actress gave the trendy piece a sophisticated spin by wearing her white crop top with a sleek and chictuxedo-style ensemble on the red carpet.

SHARON STONE, 59

The cutout bra! The lace bodysuit! The sexy tuxedo! Sharon is wearing all the staples of a street style star 30 years her junior.

JUDITH LIGHT, 68

We know that Judith loves a good form-fitting gown, but we think they should all include a sheer abs-baring window from here on out.

SUSAN SARANDON, 70

At the 2016 SAG Awards, Susan had a mother-daughter matching midriff moment with daughter Eva Amurri Martino, 32, with her black bralette top worn under a tuxedo blazer and matching trousers (all Max Mara).
