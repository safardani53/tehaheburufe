Who is Shana Wilson:

Shana Wilson is a famous Gospel Singer. She was born on January 20, 1985 and her birthplace is Detroit, MI. Shana is also well known as, Singer and songwriter known for her worship albums, she is a worship leader and choir director for the Community Christian Fellowship Church. Some of her most memorable songs include “Press In Your Presence”, “You are Holy”, and “Lion of Judah.”

Shana is originated from United States. She placed first in the 2006 edition of Vickie Winans’ “New Rising Star” contest.
