Getting a good night’s sleep is not only beneficial for your health — it sure makes for better days, too. And part of ensuring you fall (and stay) asleep easily is finding the right pillow. Some options can be too hot, while others feel too thin or too lumpy. 

If you’ve been searching for the perfect pillow, whether you’re a hot or cool sleeper, you’re in luck. The set of two cooling memory foam pillows was deemed the best budget foam pillow and earned top marks on quality, breathability, durability, and value from our PEOPLE Tested team. Our team tested 20 pillows to evaluate a number of factors, including comfort and overall value.

The queen-sized set includes two memory foam pillows that each have two different sides: One side is covered in a cooling silk fabric, and the other in a bamboo-blend fabric for a soft touch with a warm feel. 

“This pillow was amazing when it came to temperature adjustments,” one tester shared. They also appreciated that it was easy to flip the pillow over during the night when they felt they were getting too warm. They added, “I love that it feels so refreshing, especially on the cooling side.” 

The bamboo side has its advantages too, like keeping your pillow right where you want it instead of slip-sliding around. Our tester also appreciated the bamboo blend was “warm and comforting.” Plus, you can customize the size of the pillow by adjusting the fill. Want a firmer feel? Add some more fill.
