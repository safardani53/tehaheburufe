Grouplove is an American alternative rock group formed in 2009 by Hannah Hooper, Christian Zucconi, Daniel Gleason, Andrew Wessen and Benjamin Homola. They are also known for their single "Tongue Tied". Their debut album, Never Trust a Happy Song, was also produced by Rabin and released worldwide on September 13, 2011. In 2021, Grouplove released five studio albums and two EPs.
