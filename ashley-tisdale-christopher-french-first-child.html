When did High School Musical star Ashley Tisdale give birth?

HIGH School Musical star Ashley Tisdale first announced that she is expecting a baby with husband Christopher French last fall.

The star posted a photo on Instagram on September 17, 2020 of the couple and her bump as they stood in a field and fan are wondering if she gave birth.

When did High School Musical star Ashley Tisdale give birth?

Ashley gave birth to a baby girl on March 23, 2021.

The actress posted a black-and-white photo of the baby she had with Christopher French. Her name is Jupiter Iris French.

Ashley's friends and co-stars flooded the comments with their excitement, including High School Music alum Vanessa Hudgens who wrote: “Just the freaking cutest.”

Haylie Duff also gushed: “Been waiting for this!!! ✨✨✨✨ Can’t wait! Best mama & papa combo!”

Who is Christopher French?

Christopher French, 38, is married to High School Musical star Ashley Tisdale.

He is a music composer and multi-instrumentalist based in Los Angeles, California. 

He has also been in the film Unlovable, and the TV series Young & Hungry.

Christopher is the founder of alternative rock band Annie Automatic.

When did they get married? 

Christopher married Ashley in September 2014 in a rustic villa in California. 

They began dating in 2012 and he proposed to her after eight months on top of the Empire State Building in New York City.  

Christopher honored his relationship when they marked six years married saying: "Six years. There are so many moments, so many tiny fragile little moments that I can look at specifically. 

“Amazing, beautiful bright ones and hard, cloudy difficult ones... we just keep getting closer.

“I really can’t imagine life without you @ashleytisdale.

"I love you endlessly, Happy Anniversary (fav photo by @eliastahan thank you brother)," he captioned the post.

Do they have kids? 

The couple announced that they were expecting their first child together on Thursday, September 17, via an Instagram post. 

Ashley posted a picture of them staring at her baby bump in a field followed by another one of them laughing as she held her belly. 

Christopher reshared a similar photo standing behind Ashley on Instagram to announce the news to his followers.

What did fans say?

Fans and celebrities began commenting on the news saying: “This made me smile SO big.”

One follower said: “CONGRATS QUEEN.”

Vanessa Hudgens chimed in on the post and wrote: "Just the freaking cutest."

Ashley had previously opened up to People magazine in summer 2019 explaining that she wanted to wait until the right time to start a family.

She said: "For me, it's like, it's okay to not want to start a family right away. 

"People are always asking me about that, but it's just not the right time."

In her Instagram stories, the actress added that her husband “is the hottest baby daddy.”
