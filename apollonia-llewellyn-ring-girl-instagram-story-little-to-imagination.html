RING girl Apollonia Llewellyn left very little to the imagination with her latest posts on her Instagram story.

She uploaded a picture showcasing an extremely low cut grey crop top to her story and followed it up with a photo of her in a camouflage bikini.

Llewellyn shot to fame when she was signed up by KSI's agency Misfits to become a ring girl for YouTuber boxing matches.

She has since then used the exposure to build a considerable social media presence.

Her Instagram following has shot up in recent months and now sits at 559,000 followers.

That has since been overtaken by her Tiktok which sits at 690k followers.
