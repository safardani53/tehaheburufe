Eight apps that are guaranteed to recharge your sexual batteries in the bedroom

WE are so in love with our phones that almost half of us prefer them to SEX.

A poll by gadget insurance firm Loveit Coverit found a shocking 42 per cent spend more time online in bed than diving under the duvet with our partners.

So why not combine the two to put the spark back in your relationship?

Sexpert Annabelle Knight says: “Apps are great for encouraging you to try new things in bed. Gifting the power of what you do sexually to an app can be freeing.

“The latest trend is for Autonomous Sensory Meridian Response (ASMR) apps, which explore the body’s reaction to sounds.”

Ready to sexperiment? Bella Battle looks at eight apps that are guaranteed to recharge your sexual batteries.

Kindu

(Available on iOS and Android, Free)

SCARED to tell your man the kinky stuff you’d really like him to do? Kindu takes the pressure off telling him face to face.

Ideas for bedroom fun pop up on your screen and you swipe right if you want to do them, left if you don’t.

When both you and your partner say yes to something, such as sex alfresco, Kindu lets you know.

Feeld

(Available on iOS and Android, Free)

ONCE simply a threesomes app, Feeld is now like Tinder for the sexually curious.

You can post a profile as a single or couple and the app matches you with local people keen to try the same things in bed.

Threesomes and swingers fun awaits or you could try similar app 3Fun, which has over 1.5million users worldwide.

Tingles

(Available on iOS and Android, Free)

READY for a spot of aural sex? This ASMR app is chiefly designed for relaxation, but some sounds will get the sexual hotspots of your brain and body going.

Certain sounds played at the right frequency can mimic the brainwaves that initiate arousal.

So, in theory, you can just pop on your headphones and be turned on in seconds.

We-connect by We-Vibe

(Available on iOS and Android, Free)

SEX toy firm We-Vibe lets you sync a vibrator (£159.99 on lovehoney.co.uk) with your partner’s phone via app.

He can then control your pleasure from thousands of miles away at the touch of a button.

You can customise the pattern of rhythms set and vibration choices include Echo, Pulse and Cha-cha-cha.

Desire

(Available on iOS and Android, Free)

IF Trivial Pursuit isn’t really doing it for you these days, why not play Desire instead?

This cheeky app game lets you earn points by completing and challenging your other half to over 70,000 sexy dares, including role play and going commando, across categories from Dress Code to Outdoors.

Kinkoo

(Available on iOS and Android, Free)

THIS is a kinky little app designed to get you in touch with your inner Christian Grey or Anastasia Steele.

A community of adventurous and like-minded people are on hand to chat to, interact with or offer tips.

And all you have to do is upload a profile pic and a brief description of the fetishes you’d like to explore.

Vibease

(Available on iOS and Android, Free)

A BIT like We-Connect, this app helps you sync your sex toys to a partner’s phone. But the bit we love best is the erotic fiction marketplace.

You can listen to kinky stories submitted by users to get you in the mood and your vibrator will buzz in time to the sexy bits.

Who doesn’t love a bedtime story, eh?

(Available on iOS, Free)

DESIGNED to help you experiment with your partner, 69 Places is like a bucket list for places to have sex before you die. Sort of.

Categories such as In The Home lead to choices including attic and fireplace. All a bit silly but also a good excuse to try something new out of the bedroom.

We’ll be steering clear of the portaloo option, though.
