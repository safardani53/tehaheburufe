Does another person hold the other half to this broken heart tattoo? We need answers, Harry! The 21-year-old was spotted spending his off time shirtless and by the pool in Chicago on Aug. 23 before One Direction’s show later that night. While it’s normal to see Harry with all those tattoos on his body, in an up close picture, the singer actually revealed a new piece of ink added to his collection. Harry hasn’t really been linked with anyone serious lately. The furthest he’s gone with a lady is following her on Instagram because he thinks she’s cute! However, hearts don’t necessarily have to represent something romantic.

The meaning of the tattoo could be related to One Direction’s song, “Half A Heart,” from their Midnight Memories album! However, it doesn’t really add up. Considering that Midnight Memories came out in 2013, why would it take Harry two years to permanently add half a heart to his body if that song meant something to him? It could also be a reminder of how he and the rest of the One Direction guys broke their fans hearts when plans for their break were announced.

One Direction: The Truth About Their Bad Blood & Solo Albums

Don’t worry, Directioners, the band isn’t splitting up for good! Now that the case of if One Direction is breaking up for good or not is solved, we just need to know about Harry’s new broken heart tattoo!
