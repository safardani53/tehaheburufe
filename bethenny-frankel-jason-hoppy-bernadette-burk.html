Bethenny Frankel's Mother Publicly Slams Her Marriage Hollywood Life

April 25, 2024
blog

During the second season of Bethenny Ever After, the 41-year-old Skinnygirl creator has been more open about her relationship struggles. In last week’s episode, Bethenny and Jason received a counseling session aboard a boat. “We’re off course,” Bethenny revealed. “That’s what we’re doing in our lives: we keep having the same argument over and over again.”

Even though Bethenny and Jason sought professional help to resolve their marriage problems, Bernadette is not convinced that the relationship will last.” She doesn’t have any respect for marriage because she has never seen good marriages,” Bernadette explains. “She cheated on her first husband after her first couple of months of marriage.”

“She would never have married Jason but she wanted to have a child and the timing was right. She got what she wanted out of this, she married because of public opinion.”

Sadly, the scathing insults didn’t stop there. Bernadette also attacked Bethenny’s parenting skills, claiming that “she has always hired people to take care of the baby, she doesn’t take care of that child, she does photo shoots.”

Never one to back away from conflict, Bethenny is set to confront Bernadette in an upcoming episode of Bethenny Ever After in which she will viciously state that she “hates her!”
