Rita Oras Red Carpet Style: See Her Best Fashion Looks

March 19, 2024
blog

Rita Ora’s red carpet looks are just as stellar as her lyrics.

The “For You” singer never shies away from sultry moments. She always delivers the unexpected on the red carpet — from daring silhouettes to bold color combos.

In a January 2022 interview with Vogue, the “Let Me Love You” singer shared that her fashion inspirations are “Cher, Madonna, and Tina Turner.” The British pop star continued, “Their evolution as artists consistently inspires me to take risks with music, fashion, and performing.”

The “For You” singer is known to take fashion risks herself. From ball gowns covered in 3-D butterflies to thigh-high slits, Ora slays ultra-glam looks. She can rock casual ensembles with equal ease — like a head-to-toe double denim look from Louis Vuitton and a silk pajama Prada set.

One of the “You Only Love Me” musician’s most talked about looks came at the 2021 Met Gala. That year, the theme of the exhibit was In America: The Lexicon of Fashion, and the “How We Do” singer slayed in a Prada dress. The silver number included a bedazzled bra top and floor length skirt. She added even more bling to the getup with diamond necklaces and earrings by De Beers Jewelers.

For glam that night, Ora beamed with a dewy face, pink eyeshadow, blushed cheeks and glossy lips. Her blonde locks were parted down the side and twisted into a slicked back bun, finished with a sparkly clip pinned above her ear.

Another one of the “How To Be Lonely” singer’s most standout looks came at the 2022 Fashion Awards. Ora commanded attention in a Nensi Dojaka sheer outfit. She looked super stylish in a red silky bra — including two heart shaped rings at the enclosure — and strappy thong. Attached to the base of her bra was a sheer crimson layer cascading to the floor. To make the look even sexier, The Voice alum sported a pair of ruby stiletto heels.

Ora’s bold look didn’t stop there — the “Girls” artist also participated in the bleached brow trend for the night. She even rocked a mermaid inspired makeup look, with her hair styled in beachy waves and gill makeup surrounding her eyes. She kept her eye makeup simple with light mascara and pink eyeshadow.
