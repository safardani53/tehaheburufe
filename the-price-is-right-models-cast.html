Who are The Price is Right models? Meet the cast

March 18, 2024
blog

BELOVED game show The Price is Right has captured the attention of viewers since its debut in 1972.

The show has seen an array of cast changes over the years and now fans want to know who are the models showcasing the prizes.

Who is The Price is Right host?

On July 24, 2007, Drew Carey was named the new host of The Price is Right after longtime host Bob Barker retired in June of that year.

Born in Cleveland, Ohio, on May 23, 1958, made his acting debut in 1993 when he released his comedy special Drew Carey: Human Cartoon.

In 1995, Drew received his own TV series called The Drew Carey Show which ran until 2004.

While filming his show, Drew was a co-host on the 1998 comedy series Whose Line Is It Anyway? alongside Ryan Stiles, Wayne Brady, and Colin Mochrie.

Who are The Price is Right models?

The Price is Right gives audience members a chance to guess the price of certain products and the winner who guesses the closest amount takes home a prize.

The prizes are presented by a handful of models who have become familiar faces over the years — let's take a look.

Manuela Arbelaez

Born in Medellín, Colombia, Manuela Arbalaez moved to the United States to pursue a career in modeling and acting.

She became a finalist on The Price is Right Model Search in 2008 before joining the show the following year in 2009.

Amber Lancaster

Before joining The Price is Right in 2008, Amber Lancaster worked as a cheerleader for the NFL's Seattle Seahawks.

In addition to her game show gig, Amber also co-starred on MTV's The Hard Times of RJ Berger.

She also made appearances on soaps such as The Bold and the Beautiful and Days of Our Lives.

Devin Goda

Born on May 5, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Devin Goda began a career playing collegiate football at Slippery Rock University.

Following his college career, Devin signed with the Baltimore Ravens as a free agent.

Following an injury, Devin retired from football and pursued a career in modeling. In 2018, Devin joined The Price is Right.

Rachel Reynolds

Rachel Reynolds holds the title as the longest model on The Price is Right joining the show in 2003.

Prior to her time on the game show, Rachel attended Lousiana State University where she was a member of the track team.

In 2010, Rachel married her longtime boyfriend, former MLB outfielder David Dellucci. They welcomed their first child together in 2013.

James O'Halloran

After winning the Male Model Search, James was offered a job as the show's model in 2014.

Before his current career, James worked as an industrial designer in his hometown of Melbourne, Australia.

Like many of the models on the show, James also ventured into the soap world appearing on The Young and the Restless.

Alexis Gaube

Alexis Gaube is the latest to join The Price is Right family having joined in 2021.

Prior to her current gig, Alexis studied apparel merchandising and product design at Bowling Green State University.

She has also appeared in an array of ads for companies such as Target, Warby Parker, and many more.

Fans can watch new episodes of the season on weekdays on CBS at 11 a.m.
