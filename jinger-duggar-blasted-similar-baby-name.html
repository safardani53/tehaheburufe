Jinger Duggar blasted by fans for giving daughter Evangeline a name too similar to her sister Joy-An

May 06, 2024
blog

DUGGAR fans have criticized new mom Jinger for naming her daughter Evangeline, a name that is incredibly similar to two of her sister's babies.

Jinger welcomed Evangeline Jo on Sunday November 22, and fans were quick to congratulate the reality tar and her husband Jeremy Vuolo.

But others wondered why they would pick a name so close in similarity to others in the family.

Jinger's sister Jessa has a daughter named Ivy, and Joy-Anna has a daughter named Evelyn, nicknamed Evy.

"I have hope they call her Evie! They when she is with her cousins they will have Evy, Evie, and Ivy," joked one fan, as another commented: "I guess we know they’ve never bothered to read even as much as a baby name book."

"Isn't Evangeline a bit close to Evelyn? Oh I forgot this is the family with a Jana, Joy Anna and Johannah. Carry on..." added one fan.

Others reminded followers that "similiar names are even kind of a theme for the family."

Patriarch Jim Bob named his 19 children with J names.

Jinger, 26, and Jeremy, 33, told TODAY that their baby girl arrived “quickly and safely.” 

The couple told the outlet: “We’ve got good news! Jinger is recovering very well, enjoying finally being able to hold her little angel."

Evangeline weighed a healthy seven pounds. 

The couple explained the name Evangeline means “good news,” while her middle name Jo is in honor of Jeremy, whose middle name is Joseph. 

The Counting On stars are also parents to daughter Felicity, 2. 

They said of the siblings: “As for Felicity, she is in love with her little sister but has no idea how much sharing is in her future. We are so grateful to God for this beautiful gift!”

Jinger shared a first photo of her daughter on Instagram Wednesday.

The reality star captioned a photo kissing her baby girl's head: "Evangeline Jo Vuolo has arrived! 🎀 We are so thankful to God for this precious little angel!❣️"

Jinger and Jeremy announced her pregnancy to her family on the most recent season of Counting On during a virtual gathering.
