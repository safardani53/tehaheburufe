Dr. Laura Slams Miley Cyrus as &quot;Disgusting, Piece of $hit Whore!&quot;

February 21, 2024
blog

Miley Cyrus likely went too far at the MTV Video Music Awards, desperately trying to prove herself as edgy and mature.

Was her routine racist? We’ll leave that for critics to debate.

Was it worthy of the torrential criticism rained down upon her by Dr. Laura Schlessinger? Probably not.

The Sirius radio host – who was fired in 2010 for dropping the N word – ripped Miley’s provocative performance yesterday, saying on air:

“There is a place for the Miley Cyrus’ of this world… and it’s in whorehouses and bars! And if you are a parent who takes your kids to see her, you are a moron and you should lose your parental rights!”

 

But the doctor wasn’t finished.

She referred to Cyrus as a “disgusting little pig” and a “piece of shit” and added:

“Miley Cyrus is a piece of scum. Unlike Madonna who plays to adults, Miley positions herself with kids and any parent who takes their kid to a Cyrus concert should lose custody of their children.”

Laura then quoted Billy Ray Cyrus’ defense of his daughter and took it upon herself to address the parents of Liam Hemsworth:

“No decent family would want their son to marry a girl like Miley. Are you listening Mr. and Mrs. Hemsworth?”

It’s worth noting: Miley Cyrus is 20 years old. Dr. Laura Schlessinger is 66 years old.

Was Miley’s performance really deserving of THIS kind of response? From a woman three times her age? Debate and decide now!
