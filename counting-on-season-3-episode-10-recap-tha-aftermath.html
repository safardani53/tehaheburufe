Counting On Season 3 Episode 10 Recap: The Aftermath

May 15, 2024

There’s a lot to talk about in Northwest Arkansas, and on Counting On Season 3 Episode 10, the Duggars pulled up a chair to do just that.

Well, more like 19 chairs. Or would it be 22? Anyway …

If you watch Jill & Jessa Counting On online, you’re already caught up on all the goings on with the family, but let’s hear it in their own words:

Watch Jill & Jessa Counting On Season 3 Episode 10 Online

Disappointing as it is that we were forced to sit through yet another clip show, there was plenty to go over with the TLC show’s producers.

First up? Jessa and Ben Seewald’s pregnancy news.

"I am 17 and a half weeks along, and I’m feeling great," Jessa reported, glowing. "I think with the first pregnancy, it seemed a lot longer."

"Then we were counting down the weeks, the months, until the baby would be here, and this time around we are chasing little Spurgeon."

That addition will certainly change things, to be sure.

"So I think the weeks are going by a lot faster," she said, and looking back, "I can’t believe we’re almost to the halfway mark already."

As for whether Jessa Duggar is expecting twins?

At this point, she has yet to have an ultrasound.

Even if she did know the gender, or genders, it doesn’t sound like we’ll find out until the baby (or babies) join Ben, Jessa and L’il Spurge.

"We might just keep it to ourselves," Ben said.

Another theme touched on Tuesday night was how dangerous life was for Jill and Derick Dillard in Central America with their son, Israel.

"One night we were getting ready for bed, and we heard this loud crashing sound," Jill said, tearing up as she recalled rushing to her "safe room."

Where was that, exactly? And what happened?!

She and Israel hid in the bathroom, messaging people to pray for them while Derick bravely checked out what had caused the noise …

… which was just a shower rack falling down.

Pretty lame story, building it up so dramatically only to have it amount to nothing, but we have no doubt that life in El Salvador was dicey.

As for rumors that Jill Duggar is pregnant again?

Not yet. Give it time, DD said. Actually, his exact words were "not yet," which is kind of an odd way of putting it, but nonetheless clear.

Meanwhile, Jeremy Vuolo arrived to surprise Jinger Duggar and delighted in recalling the details of their courtship for the 1,259th time …

Regarding their relatively racy relationship and habit of shattering courting rules, Jeremy said that the couple has its boundaries, but …

Well, they just want to paw each other!

Said the handsome Texas pastor:

"Jinger and I are both very affectionate … It means a lot to have an arm around her or something, and that’s how we express our affection."

"We have a clean conscience as long, as it’s not leading us to impurity," Vuolo said, no doubt needling Jim Bob a little with that quote.

Jinger and Jeremy were also grilled about their post-marriage plans, from starting a family to Jinger leaving Arkansas for the Lone Star State.
