Ive found the best place in London for a family day out - theres trampolines, soft play and it

September 21, 2024

A MOTHER has revealed that she has found the best place in London for a fun, family day out.

Not only is it great for children of all ages, but it’s super cheap too. 

So if you are struggling to find activities to keep your little one busy this summer, you’ll need to listen up. 

Tameka Milian took her son to Oxygen - an indoor free jumping park - for a day full of bouncing on trampolines and fun in the soft play. 

Posting about her fun day out on social media, she said: “Places to visit with children in London, Freejump Trampoline Park and soft play.

“Perfect for all ages of the family.”

Tameka then added: “This place is a must-visit with children and is the perfect family day out because it’s fun for everyone. 

“There’s a huge trampoline area and an obstacle course for older children and a huge soft play area for younger children and toddlers.

“There’s also an arcade and a cafe which is very reasonably priced. 

“My son had so much fun, I would definitely recommend you checking them out.”

While there are many Oxygen locations around London, Tameka confirmed that she took her little boy to the Oxygen at The O2. 

When asked how much it costs to go, Tameka confirmed that it is free for adults and just £4 for toddlers. 

If you have older children and want to do a 60 minute free jump session, it will cost you £15, whereas a 90 minute free jump session is £19.

Tameka’s TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @tameka.milian, has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly amassed 108,600 views. 

Social media users were stunned at the epic day out and many were eager to express this in the comments. 

One person said: “We need to visit this one. Looks huge and loads of fun.”

Another added: “Was here on Friday with my daughter, she enjoyed it so much!” 

A third commented: “Our little one would love it here!” 

To this, Tameka replied: “Definitely! We’ve been twice already now.” 

One user asked: “Are toddlers allowed to use the trampolines?”, to which Tameka confirmed “Yes, it’s just priced separately.”
