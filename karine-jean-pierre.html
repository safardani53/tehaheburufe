Former Vice President Joe Biden’s presidential campaign for the November 2020 election is hitting the ground running, thanks to his dedicated team which includes Senior Advisor, Karine Jean -Pierre. Pierre, 42, is a Haitian-American campaign organizer, who previously worked on campaigns for John Edwards, Martin O’Malley and Barack Obama and served in President Obama’s White House. She was raised in Queens, New York, currently lives in Washington DC and is mom to a 6 year-old daughter.She has also worked as a senior advisor for MoveOn.org,  a progressive political group, is the author of Moving Forward and regularly appears on cable news shows on MSNBC and NBC News as a political analyst.

Jean- Pierre calls the 2020 presidential election, ‘ the most consequential election of our lifetimes’ in an exclusive interview with Hollywoodlife.com. She also describes it as the nost challenging because of Covid- 19. ‘ We are running a campaign that’s never been done before- a virtual campaign and against the type of opponent ( Donald Trump) that no one has ever seen before’. But she is fully confident that ‘ Joe Biden is up for it. I think we ( the Biden tcampaign team) are all up for it. We are going to make it happen. We have to so that’s what we are doimng every day.” Jean- Pierre confesses that she is ‘ fearful for the country I love and fearful for my daughter and the people of this country, if we do not take back the White House on November 3rd.”Pictured here, Pierre poses before a talk at the 92nd Street Y on October 7,2018 in New York.
