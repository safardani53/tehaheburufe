20. Michael Bisping (Net Worth: $9 Million)

Michael Bisping is a retired British Mixed Martial Artist, sports analyst, commentator, and actor.

The UFC Hall of Famer fought in the UFC’s middleweight and light heavyweight divisions, becoming the first British fighter to have competed in a UFC main event (from UFC 78).

Similarly, Bisping is the first British fighter to have won a UFC Championship and stands as the only British fighter to have done so. 

As of 2022, Michael Bisping is the 20th wealthiest MMA fighter in the World, with a net worth of $9 Million.

19. Jose Aldo (Net Worth: $9 Million)

José Aldo da Silva Oliveira Jr. currently ranks #4 in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) bantamweight rankings.

The Brazilian Mixed Martial Artist, named The 2009 Fighter of the Year by The Sherdog, transitioned to bantamweight in 2019.

He is also the former UFC featherweight champion overall, having won the title thrice.

Similarly, Aldo remained undefeated for a decade from 2005 to 2015.

In addition, he won 18 straight fights for the time being.

As of 2022, Jose Aldo’s net worth is estimated to be $9 million, making him the 19th richest MMA fighter in the World.

18. Donald Cerrone (Net Worth: $9.5 Million)

Donald Antony Cerrone, also known as Cowboy, currently fights in the Welterweight Division of the UFC.

The American Mixed Martial Artist holds the record of having most bouts in the UFC at 37.

Similarly, he grabbed the record of having the most wins at UFC with his 23 victories.

He also has the highest number of post-fight bonus awards having 18 of them, and the most knockdowns with 20 in the UFC.

Donald Cerrone currently has a net worth of $9.5 Million, making him the 18th richest MMA fighter in the World.

He has been an actor in the movies; Terror on the Prairie, The Commando, The Equalizer 2, Embattled, etc.

Junior dos Santos Almeida is a former UFC heavyweight champion. The UFC released him on March 3, 2021.

The Brazilian Mixed Martial Artist had victories over some of the toughest MMA fighters.

He paved the path from small Brazil-based promotion to UFC, working as a professional MMA fighter for almost 15 years.

As of 2022, Junior Dos Santos has a net worth of $10 Million. He stands firm as the 17th wealthiest MMA fighter in the World.

Similarly, Sonnen is one of the best MMA analysts who now indulges in Submission Underground (SUG), his venture for a submission wrestling promotion.

The former UFC Light Heavyweight Champion, Charles David Liddell, first retired in December 2010.

Chuck Liddel is the 12th wealthiest MMA fighter in the World as of 2022, having a net worth of $12 Million.

11. Ronda Rousey (Net Worth: $13 Million)

Ronda Jean Rousey is a retired American Mixed Martial Artist. She is also a professional wrestler and a judoka.

She was the UFC women’s bantamweight champion and held the record of having the most UFC title defenses for women.

Likewise, she is the first female fighter to get inducted into the UFC Hall of Fame.

She also has a pretty successful WWE career, winning the Raw Women’s Championship.

Moreover, she is the first female fighter to have won both WWE and UFC Championships.

As of 2022, Ronda Rousey is the 11th richest MMA fighter in the world, with a net worth of $13 Million.
