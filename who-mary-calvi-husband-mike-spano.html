CBS news anchor Mary Calvi is a well-known face on the network and anchors morning news in New York.

Calvi's husband Mike Spano is currently serving as the 42nd Mayor of Yonkers, New York.

How did Mary Calvi and Mike Spano meet?

According to Wag Mag, Calvi and Spano met while Spano was running the Yonkers Columbus Day Parade and Calvi was Miss Columbus.

"Our eyes met then, but Mary was off to college," Spano told the magazine.

"I asked her out soon after she graduated and the rest, as they say, is history," he continued.

What are some of Mike Spano's accomplishments?

Mike Spano was elected in 1992 as the youngest member of the state assembly. He has been serving as the Mayor of Yonkers since 2018.

Since his early days of being involved in government, Mayor Spano has led the Task Force on Sex Crimes Against Children and Women, fought for the passage of Megan’s Law, and secured legislation to protect children across the state.

Do Mary Calvi and Mike Spano have children?

Spano and Calvi have three children together– Alexandra, Michael and Christopher.

Spano is a big family man. Being that he is ninth out of a 16-sibling family, Mayor Spano understands the importance of dedicating time to his family.
