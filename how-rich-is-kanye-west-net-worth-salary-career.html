How Rich Is Kanye West? Net Worth, Salary, Career

February 29, 2024

Soon after Gap revealed its impending partnership with Balenciaga, Kanye West decided to call out the company for reportedly having a meeting without him. The rapper and Gap first announced their Yeezy Gap collaboration in 2020, but their conditions seem to be deteriorating. This occurred when Kanye uploaded a photo of enormous Yeezy Gap sunglasses on a table with the caption:

“Did Gap have a meeting regarding me without my presence?”

The 45-year-old rapper then responded with another Instagram post accusing Gap of ripping off his Yeezy Gap x Balenciaga line. He also claimed in the same tweet that Gap canceled a shoot with his children without even contacting West.

Is Kanye West making a fortune from his deal with Gap? Looking at the rapper’s net worth in 2023

While Kanye West may currently be disparaging the company, the reality is that this cooperation between Kanye West and Gap made him millions. This has significantly increased his net worth in recent years. Kanye Omari West, an American musician, singer, rapper, and fashion designer, has several projects in all of these fields, which have helped him increase his net worth to $6.6 billion this year. When it comes to Kanye and his music, he is one of the most successful hip-hop artists in the world. He is one of the best-selling musicians of all time, having dabbled in a variety of genres. His music was expected to be worth $110 million in 2023.

He has over 25 Grammy Awards and a massive number of monthly Spotify listeners. The application ends up paying the rapper more than $15 million every year. He also receives equivalent amounts from other music streaming applications such as Apple Music and Amazon Music. Aside from that, he is a rapper who earns millions of dollars through his tours and musical excursions. The tours have managed to make him more than $200 million in the previous several years. Furthermore, the rapper owns his own label, GOOD Music, which earns him a few million dollars each year.

When it comes to his enterprises, notably in the fashion industry, he has his own brand called Yeezy. This has resulted in more than $200 million in revenue through partnerships and collaborations, including his work with Adidas and Gap. Kanye West has a large number of properties, one of which is his $57.3 million Malibu beach property. Other multi-million dollar assets include additional houses and a number of luxury vehicles such as the Lamborghini Urus, Mercedes SLR, and Bugatti Veyron. With a private aircraft and a boat of his own, Kanye West is typically believed to be one of the wealthiest musicians in the nation.

Kanye accuses Gap of ‘copying’ Yeezy Gap

West’s accusation that Gap was ignoring him and duplicating his brand’s look came less than 24 hours after Yeezy Gap revealed photographs of its next campaign with prominent designer Balenciaga. Leather zip-up coats, long rubber boots, and skin-tight shoes are among the items in the collection.

Simultaneously, his line, Yeezy Gap Engineered by Balenciaga, has grown in popularity and success. Kanye West’s desire to experiment with styles and trends as a designer may have contributed to the collection’s appeal. Gap has not yet responded to the claim that it copied Kanye West’s brand.
