Paramount+ And Nickelodeon Announce Star-Studded Voice Cast for Baby Shark's Big Movie!

March 13, 2024
blog

Ashley Tisdale, Cardi B, Offset and Kulture among lineup of guest voice actors in the new original animated film.

More fintastic news is swimming your way! Paramount+ and Nickelodeon announced their star-studded lineup of guest voice actors for their feature-length original animated film, Baby Shark's Big Movie!

In Baby Shark's Big Movie!, Baby Shark is forced to leave the world he loves behind after his family's move to the big city and must adjust to his new life without his best friend, William. When Baby Shark encounters an evil pop starfish named Stariana, who plans to steal his gift of song to dominate all underwater music, he must break her spell to restore harmony to the seas.

STREAM: Baby Shark's Big Show! On Paramount+

Actress, singer and producer Ashley Tisdale (Phineas and Ferb the Movie: Candace Against the Universe, High School Musical) joins the cast as Stariana, a sharp-witted and manipulative rising pop starfish who wants to take center stage with a splash hit; Aparna Nancherla (Mira, Royal Detective) as Gillie, a charmingly chaotic, bumbling boxfish, and Stariana's assistant; Ego Nwodim (Saturday Night Live) as Leah, a lantern shark and Mommy Shark's best friend from college; Chloe Fineman (Saturday Night Live, Father of the Bride) as Lannie, Leah's hyper, lovable daughter; ENHYPEN as an underwater powerhouse K-pop band of belugas; and Lance Bass as TV announcer Lance Bass.

The new voice actors will join returning guest stars from the hit series Baby Shark's Big Show!: Grammy Award-winning, multi-platinum superstar Cardi B as rap icon Sharki B, and her family - Offset as Offshark, Kulture as Kulture Sharki and newcomer Wave as Wavey Shark.

The movie will also star the swim-sational lineup of series regulars from Baby Shark's Big Show!: Kimiko Glenn (Orange Is the New Black) as Baby Shark; Luke Youngblood (Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone) as William; Natasha Rothwell (Insecure) as Mommy Shark; Eric Edelstein (We Bare Bears) as Daddy Shark; Debra Wilson (MADtv) as Grandma Shark; and Patrick Warburton (Seinfeld) as Grandpa Shark.

The upcoming film based on the globally beloved preschool property is set to premiere later this year exclusively on Paramount+ in the U.S. and shortly after on Paramount+ internationally, where the service is available. In Korea, Southeast Asia and China, Baby Shark's Big Movie! is distributed by The Pinkfong Company.

Baby Shark's Big Movie! is co-produced by Nickelodeon Animation and The Pinkfong Company and directed by Daytime Creative Arts Emmy Award-winner Alan Foreman (The Casagrandes, Welcome to the Wayne).

The series launched globally in 2021 across Nickelodeon branded channels and platforms. Stream the first season of the hit preschool series Baby Shark's Big Show! on Paramount+.
