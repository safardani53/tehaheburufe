Green Bay Packers quarterback Aaron stunned fans back in February when he thanked his "fiancée" during an MVP acceptance speech.

This came after just days prior, rumors began to swirl that Aaron was dating Shailene - though neither party had officially confirmed their relationship at the time.

The pair had kept their romance a secret, and Shailene recently let slip that this was because they made a pact not tell anyone.

Speaking to The Hollywood Reporter she confessed: "When we announced that we were engaged, we wanted to do that only because we didn't want someone else to do it before we did.

"And we didn't do it for months and months after we had become engaged, but the reaction to it was really a lot, and so we were like, ‘Let's just politely decline [to talk about the relationship] for a little while and live in our little bubble.'"

According to the magazine, the two were introduced through mutual friends, both musicians.

They grew their bond during the pandemic, moving in together within a few months and traveling back and forth between California and Wisconsin.

"You could travel, but you had masks on," Shailene recalled. "There was a sense of anonymity that otherwise I don't think we would have had."

The Divergent actress continued: "We were really able to get to know one another the way we wanted to get to know one another and not have any noise or chaos around us."
