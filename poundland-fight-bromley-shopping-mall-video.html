THIS is the horrifying moment Poundland prize fighters batter each other last night with shopping baskets outside a discount store in Bromley, South East London.

Astonishing footage shows a brawl break out directly outside the shop shortly before police were called to a shoplifting incident.

The clip starts with a man in a black shirt repeatedly punching another man in a grey shirt after knocking him to the ground.

The beaten man tries to stand up but the man bashes him down again and kicks him on the ground.

A woman wearing a big hat then whacks her aggressor over the head with a shopping basket - only for the grey-shirted man to start hitting two other onlookers.

The woman circles the four men with a shopping basket, bashing a man in a blue shirt.

Other shoppers stop to watch as the black and grey-shirted men continue their bizarre shopping basket battles.

An onlooker in a pink shirt then tries to intervene but to no avail.

It is unclear who started the fight or what it is about.

The woman is heard saying "You hit me and I'm pregnant" as she continues trying to hit people with the basket.

In the clip, the men in blue and pink appear to be trying to stop the other three fighting but to little effect.

The man filming is heard saying at the end: "It's all kicked off in Poundland," adding: "Buy one get one free."

A spokeswoman for Poundland told Sun Online: “We take colleague safety very seriously.

"We called police to a shoplifting incident at our Bromley store on Friday evening and they arrested two individuals for theft and assault.

"We’re aware of the video and are investigating precisely what happened at the store.”
