DWTS fans 'know' which celeb will win new season after frontrunner shocks judges with high score on

September 30, 2024
blog

DANCING With the Stars fans are convinced that Jason Mraz will win season 32 after he gave an incredible performance.

The I'm Yours singer, 46, shocked fans with his impeccable cha cha with his dancing partner Daniella Karagach during the premiere on Tuesday.

Jason and Daniella danced to one of his own songs, I Feel Like Dancing, and were given sevens by all the DWTS judges.

Bruno Tonioli called Jason “a dancer," Carrie Ann Inaba declared that it was “the best dance of the night” and Derek Hough sait it was “spot on.”

Jason ended the night tied for second place with Ariana Madix and partner Pasha Pashkov, earning 21 out of a possible 30 points.

The two-time Grammy winner claimed in a pre-recorded segment that he has “faked his way through dancing," throughout his career.

He said: “In college, I studied musical theater, but then one day I got to the door of the dance studio and said, ‘I’m not going back in there.

"I knew what was in that room — humiliation. Because the students had a lot more experience than me and I’d see all my weaknesses.

“I just went and focused where I could focus and sing immediately, which was like busking on the sidewalk and playing in coffee shops."

Jason added: ” Since then, I’ve hid behind a guitar, but a part of me still wants to go through that door.”

Viewers went wild for Jason's performance on X, formerly known as Twitter, and predicted that he will win the competition.

One posted: "I'm calling it now Jason Mraz with the win! He can dance AND he will win everyone over with his personality!"

Another added: "Jason Mraz probably gonna win. He was actually pretty good, especially for the first night."

A third agreed, writing: "Ok, Jason Mraz being the best one on DWTS was not on my bingo card, but I kind of love it!"

Another user referenced Daniella winning DWTS with Iman Shumpert in 2021.

"Daniella winning twice in the space of 2 years I’m about to be unbearable," they wrote and added a GIF of someone celebrating.

BIDDING FAREWELL

At the end of the season 32 premiere, three couples were called back after tying for the lowest scores of the evening.

Harry Jowsey and Rylee Arnold, Matt Walsh and Koko Iwasaki, and Tyson Beckford and Jenna Johnson all faced a nervous wait as they staked their claim to remain in the competition.

It was Matt and Koko who were the first to leave the show.

However, fans wanted two controversial contestants - Adrian Peterson and Jamie Lynn Spears - to be axed.

Adrian, 38, has two counts of domestic abuse against him for violence towards his wife and then four-year-old son.

Many fans believe Jamie Lynn was involved in her sister, Britney Spears', conservatorship.

Neither of them went home nor were in the bottom two.
