Noah Centineo rose to fame thanks to his breakout role as the ultimate teen heartthrob Peter Kavinsky in the Netflix rom-com ‘To All The Boys I’ve Loved Before.’ But while he stole our hearts as the love interest to Lana Condor’s Lara Jean Covey, he didn’t stop making us swoon after the credits rolled.
