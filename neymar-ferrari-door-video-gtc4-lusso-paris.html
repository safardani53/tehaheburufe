NEYMAR was left out of this year's Ballon d'Or nominations - but that's not been his only door-related dilemma of late.

The Brazilian star struggled to open his £250,000 Ferrari GTC4 Lusso after he went out for a bite to eat in Paris.

Neymar was spotted rubbing his key against his sweater as he desperately tried to unlock his motor in the cold conditions.

Thiago Silva's wife Isabelle Silva caught the hilarious incident on camera.

She jokingly asked: "Are you trying to rob other people's cars?"

Before she advised: "Rub it!"

The 27-year-old eventually managed to open the door of his supercar after he held the key close to the vehicle.

According to Brazilian source Universo Online (UOL), the battery on the key was running low.

A Ferrari spokesman told UOL: "In emergencies, you can detach a mechanical key and insert it into the car to open it.

"In the case of Neymar, if the battery had not worked again, he could have done that."

Neymar had been out to a restaurant with team-mates Marquinhos, Silva and close friend Kevin Trapp.
