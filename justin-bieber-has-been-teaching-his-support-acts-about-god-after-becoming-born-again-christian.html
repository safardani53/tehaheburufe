JUSTIN BIEBER has hardly been a saint over the years – but is on a holy mission to change that.

I’ve learned he has been holding bible studies backstage on his world tour to teach his support acts about God.

Biebs is also trying to convert his family and friends to Christianity after becoming a “born-again” believer.

A source revealed: “Backstage on his Purpose tour, Justin has been sitting down with his opening acts and encouraging them to put Christ before everything.

“His aim is to convert the people he loves and warn other stars against the evils of the industry.

“Justin’s life changed for the better since he found God and he has been making better decisions now that he has found peace within his faith.

“After seeing so many other celebs go off the rails, he wants to help his friends find the joy and happiness he has.”

Justin first turned to religion two years ago after finding himself in all sorts of trouble, including egging his neighbour’s house and getting into a punch-up with ORLANDO BLOOM.

He turned his life around with the help of Pastor Carl Lentz. The religious leader invited him to New York for two weeks to study the Bible and attend services.

Earlier this year, the singer even got a tattoo of a cross on his face.

In interviews, Justin has not shied away from speaking about his beliefs.

He said earlier this year: “I love talking about my faith.

“Christians have left such a bad taste in people’s mouths — just like, overly pushy with the subject, overly churchy and religious.

“I think that people, as soon as they start hearing me saying I’m a Christian, they’re like, ‘Whoa, Justin! Back up, take a step back!’

“I just wanna honestly live like Jesus.”

As well as living like Jesus, I reckon Justin looks quite like him thanks to my handy mock-up.
