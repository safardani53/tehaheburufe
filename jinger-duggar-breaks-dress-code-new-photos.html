JINGER Duggar has shared a new video with her bra peeking out of her top as she continues to defy the rules of her strict parents.

In her three-part Instagram Story, Jinger spoke to the camera while wearing a yellow top that featured a V-neckline.

Her black bra could be seen peeking out through the bottom of the neckline as she continued to sell her followers on Ritual's supplements.

Jinger and Jeremy moved to Los Angeles, California in 2019.

CLOSE CONTACT

Despite the physical distance from the rest of her family, Jinger said that she is still close with many of her siblings.

"We still have close relationships."

However, Jeremy has previously hinted that the family isn't as close as it once was.

In 2021, when Jinger, along with siblings Jill, Jessa, and Joy-Anna, sued the City of Springdale, Arkansas, for invasion of privacy, Jeremy said in a deposition that there had been some "distance" between Jinger and the rest of the Duggars.

"Has there been more distance? And obviously, there's more physical distance since you moved to California. Is there more emotional distance between Jinger and her parents than there was before you moved to California?" Jeremy was asked, per court papers obtained by The U.S. Sun.
