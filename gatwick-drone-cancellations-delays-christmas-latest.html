Gatwick Airport Christmas flight cancellations and delays in full

August 05, 2024
blog

THOUSANDS of travellers were this week left facing Christmas chaos after drone sightings forced Gatwick Airport to close.

But are the disruptions still going?

Gatwick Airport has confirmed that as of Saturday December 22, the runway is open with a full schedule expected to run.

In a statement on their website, the airport confirmed: "Passengers should expect some delays and cancellations as we continue to recover our operations following three days of disruption and are advised to check with their airline before travelling to the airport.

"Safety is Gatwick’s top priority and we are grateful for passengers’ continued patience as we work to get them to their final destination in time for Christmas.

Sussex Police last night confirmed they had arrested a man and woman after raids were carried out.
