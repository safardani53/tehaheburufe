MARGOT Robbie sets pulses racing as she performs a raunchy pole dance in stockings and suspenders.

She writhes in a black bra and vamps up her look with blue lipstick - in scenes from her new film Terminal.

Margot showed off her incredible figure in her racy outfit as she oozed confidence for the scenes.

The former Neighbours star turned Hollywood actress wore her blond hair swept up in a half up, with ringlets cascading down her chest.

She was pictured alongside British funnyman Simon Pegg for the LA premiere of the film earlier this week at Arclight cinemas in Los Angeles, California.

The Suicide Squad actress covered up her fabulous figure for the occasion in a floaty dress with intricate lace floral detailing on the neck.

Terminal is about two assassins who are tasked with carrying out a high risk mission.

They are employed by an anonymous benefactor who promises them a large payout.

Over the course of the film, the hit men's lives intersect with a terminally ill teacher, a janitor and an eccentric waitress.

As the plot unravels, revenge, mystery and crime abound in the dark and disturbing thriller.
