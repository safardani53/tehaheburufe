Who is Johnny Depp's daughter Lily-Rose?

LILY-ROSE Depp is the daughter of the famous actor, Johnny Depp, and French singer, Vanessa Paradis.

Following in her father's footsteps, Lily-Rose has made a name for herself in the cinematic world.

Who is Lily-Rose Depp?

Lily-Rose Melody Depp, 24, is a French-American actress and model who was born in Paris on May 27, 1999.

She is one of three children shared with Pirates of the Caribbean actor Johnny Depp and French singer Vanessa Paradis.

In 2014, Lily-Rose began making a name for herself in Hollywood when she starred in a small role in the indie film Tusk alongside her dad.

She went on to appear in the drama The Dancer in 2016, which earned her a nomination for Most Promising Actress at the 42nd César Awards.

In 2019, Depp won a Los Angeles Film Award for Best Actress in the French short film My Last Lullaby.

As of June 2023, Depp has 15 film credits under her belt, including her lead role in the 2023 HBO original series, The Idol.

Additionally, like her mum, Depp is also a model and has been an ambassador for Chanel since 2015.
