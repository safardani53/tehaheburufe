Fans convinced Ryan Reynolds is 7ft tall as optical illusion shows Hollywood superstar towering over

October 02, 2024
blog

FOOTBALL fans are shocked by how much Hollywood star Ryan Reynolds towered over the likes of Gary Lineker and Alan Shearer.

The Wrexham co-owner featured on the BBC's coverage of Wrexham's draw with Sheffield United in the FA Cup fourth round.

And the actor looked gigantic compared to former strikers Lineker, Shearer and Mark Hughes.

One supporter watching at home was convinced Reynolds must be about seven feet.

But the cameras must have been playing tricks as Reynolds is only 6ft2.

Shearer is 6ft but the gap between them seemed much bigger, as was the case for 5ft10 Lineker.
