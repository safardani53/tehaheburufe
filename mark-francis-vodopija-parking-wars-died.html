Who was Parking Wars cast member Mark Francis Vodopija and how did he die?

February 18, 2024
blog

MARK Francis Vodopija died suddenly after appearing on A&E Network's reality show, Parking Wars.

The show follows employees of the Philadelphia Parking Authority as they go on their daily rounds.

Who is Mark Francis Vodopija?

Mark appeared on Parking Wars which premiered in 2008 and ran for seven seasons.

He was born in 1985 in Fishtown in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

He graduated from Douglass High School in 2003.

Mark worked for the Philadelphia Parking Authority, who were often seen dealing with irate customers or worker issues on the reality show.

His Parking Wars bio describes him as "a Impound lot officer who worked for the PPA.

"He is a very friendly person when it comes to helping the customer out or asking his co workers for assistance."

How did Mark Francis Vodopija die?

Mark passed away on January 22, 2009 in Fishtown at age 24.

His obituary appeared in the Philadelphia Inquirer and said that he died suddenly, while his Parking Wars bio cites his cause of death as from a "rare disease."

His funeral was held at the Holy Name of Jesus Church in Fishtown.
