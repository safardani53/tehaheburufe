Poundland is selling outdoor wine bottle and glass holders and its the perfect thing for lockdown

February 12, 2024
blog

WITH the UK in lockdown those of us lucky enough to have gardens are spending a lot of time in them as the weather warms up. 

Relaxing in the sun is now a daily ritual for people who have access to outside space, with most enjoying the outdoors with a drink in hand.

Rather than bringing a mug or pint glass outside, if you wanted to add a touch of class to your day Poundland is selling an outdoor wine and glass holder for £1.

The store has remained open during the pandemic, and people have been snapping up the nifty holder as they shop for essentials.

Sharing a snap of the holder to Facebook yesterday, Poundland said: “Cheers to the weekend!

“It's Friday and it's wine time!

“Keep it hands free with our £1 outdoor wine bottle and glass holders.”

The holders don’t just accommodate wine or champagne flutes, as you can fit bottles and other glasses in them so it’s perfect even if you’re sticking to soft drinks.

Commenting online, one person said: “What a useful item.”

Another wrote: “Sooo need these.”

A third said: “We need these for Friday lunchtimes.”

This person added: “This would look good in one of our gardens ladies.”

While another thought: “Fantastic idea!!”
