Popular BBC series with celebrity host returning to screens for a seventh series

June 17, 2024
blog

POPULAR BBC show Between the Covers looks set to return for a seventh series.

The show, all about encouraging the nation to read, is fronted by one of the Beeb's top stars, Sara Cox.

Between the Covers airs on BBC Two and will be back for another outing where a series of newly released books are discusses among a panel of famous faces.

Four famous faces discuss the topics and themes of the chosen book each episode.

The books are handpicked in order to shine a spotlight on them in the public domain.

Encompassing a range of genres, the guests all bring a book of their own that they feel deserves its moment in the limelight.

More on Sara Cox

The 'Bring Your Own Book' nature is a move created in order to inspire the viewers to do the same within their own circle of friends.

Among some of the stars who have appeared on the show are TV presenter Mel Giedroyc, actress Samantha Bond, former Love Island host Laura Whitmore, The Masked Dancer star Adam Garcia and presenter Nick Knowles.

For the authors whose work is chosen to be featured on the show, their books will feature a sticker on the front cover, when bought in shops, to alert potential buyers that it has appeared on the BBC show.

The show first launched in October 2020 with Sara at the helm.

Sara herself has taken to writing in recent years.

She published a memoir in 2019 all about her life growing up in 1980s Lancashire.

Sara then went on to pen her first novel, titled Thrown, published in May 2022.
