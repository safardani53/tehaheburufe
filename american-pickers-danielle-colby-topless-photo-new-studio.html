DROPPED RATINGS

Danielle returned with the Wolfe brothers Mike, 58, and Robbie, 62, as co-hosts for the Season 25 premiere in early July.

In mid-July, The U.S. Sun revealed the ratings hit a downward spiral and dropped by 100,000 viewers.

While 918,000 viewers watched the premiere, ratings dipped to 803,000 during the installment on July 12.

Near the end of the month, the U.S. Sun also reported that another 100,000 viewers were missing during the July 19 installment, as just 713,000 people tuned in. 

The ratings finally ticked up a bit for the July 26 episode with about 885,000 viewers watching. 

FRANK'S FIRING

While the show may have had an uptick, viewership has overall been down since Frank Fritz's firing.

The History Channel alum last appeared on American Pickers during a March 2020 episode, when he took time off to recover from back surgery.

Frank, 57, was let go from the show a year later.

Mike was the one who publicly revealed that Frank had been booted from the show in July 2021.

After he was booted from the show, Frank was placed under conservatorship after he suffered a debilitating stroke in July 2022 that reportedly left his decision­-making capacity impaired.

Frank was replaced by Mike's older brother Rob.

Despite his abrupt absence from the show and major health issues, many fans on social media would still love to see Frank come back.
