Shia LaBeouf Accused of Assaulting Girlfriend Mia Goth

October 07, 2024
blog

Shia LaBeouf has engaged in some bizarre and occasionally criminal behavior over the years, but with the help of rehab and a stellar PR team, he’s kept his career somewhat intact.

If the latest reports of his bad behavior are true, however, LaBeouf may have entered territory from which he’ll be unable to return.

Several German media outlets are reporting that LaBeouf publicly assaulted girlfriend Mia Goth yesterday.

Witnesses say the couple was involved in a loud altercation outside of a hotel, and Goth was left with a black eye following a brief physical altercation.

Locals broke up the fight and drove LaBeouf to the airport, where he reportedly claimed he would have killed Goth if he had stayed.

Sources claim LaBeouf was nursing a badly injured hand (presumably from punching a wall during the fight), and he appeared to be heavily intoxicated.

LaBeouf completed rehab last year after a strange incident in which he was arrested for disorderly conduct when he drunkenly disrupted a Broadway play.

It was reported that LaBeouf and Goth got engaged shortly after he completed treatment, but neither party has confirmed the news.

Insiders have verified that LaBeouf is back in the States, but other details – such as the extent of Goth’s injuries and the question of whether or not LaBeouf will face charges – are unknown at this time.
