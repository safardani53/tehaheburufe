Who is Paul Thorn:

Paul Thorn is a famous Gospel Singer. He was born on July 13, 1964 and his birthplace is United States. Paul is also well known as, Southern rock, blues, and gospel singer and songwriter known for his diverse range of styles within music. He is known for his work with A&M Records and Perpetual Obscurity Records and for his albums “Don’t Let the Devil Ride” and “A Long Way from Tupelo”. 

Paul is originated from United States. He has toured as an opening act for multiple artists including Jeff Beck, John Prine, and Huey Lewis and the News.
