Rachel Zoe Debuts New Maxi-Cosi and Quinny Collaboration

July 03, 2024
blog

Rachel Zoe knows fashion — she also knows the stress of being a busy mom.

In her new collection for Quinny and Maxi-Cosi, Zoe kept both aspects of her life in mind while creating a carseat and stroller that marry both “style and function.”

“Something practical can also be fashionable, which is why my collaboration with Quinny/Maxi-Cosi was such a natural fit,” Zoe, 44, tells PEOPLE exclusively.

“For me, the details are what make this collection special. Rich hues, leather stitching and gold accents add an element of glamour and sophistication.”


Courtesy of Rachel Zoe x Maxi-Cosi

The Fashionably Late with Rachel Zoe star, who has two young boys – Skyler, 4, and Kaius, 21 months – says the “chic” products are a way for parents to “say who you are without having to speak.”

“And for moms and dads to live their lives in style every day,” she tells PEOPLE. “My husband and I are so excited to start using the collection with our two boys!”


JP Yim/Getty

The collaboration, which Zoe says is meant to “compliment your look,” will be available in 2016 at various speciality retailers nationwide.


Courtesy of Rachel Zoe x Maxi-Cosi
