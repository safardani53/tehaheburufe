Real Housewives of Salt Lake City Season 4 Cast Net Worth

July 14, 2024
blog

The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City, best known as RHOSLC, is an American reality television series that began on Bravo on November 11, 2020.

It aired three seasons as the tenth iteration of The Real Housewives franchise and focuses on the personal and professional lives of numerous women living in or around Salt Lake City, Utah. Currently, RHOSLC is in its fourth season.

The production of the Season 4 began in March 2023. However, it premiered on September 5, 2023.

Lisa Barlow, Heather Gay, Meredith Marks, Whitney Rose, Monica Garcia, and Angie Katsanevas are among the current fourth-season cast members, with former housewife Mary Cosby acting as a Friend of the Housewives.

Since the premiere of the show, people have been curious about the housewives and their lives. As a result, they often want to know the RHOSLC season 4 cast net worth.

Here is all we know about the cast of RHOSLC, their net worth, along with their biography, profession, and personal life.

Lisa Barlow

Barlow was born and raised in New York City. She became a Mormon in New York and believes it to be one of the best decisions she has ever made. 

According to Barlow, being a Mormon is all about having a more aware sense of self, and she believes in inclusivity and not judging others. As a result, she no longer adheres to all of the Mormon practices she formerly did.

After graduation, the New York native relocated to the Salt Lake area, claiming she fell in love with the area. She has since concentrated on making her aspirations a reality. 

For over two decades, Barlow has resided in Salt Lake City, Utah, with her husband, John, and their two children, Jack and Henry. While she is best known as the cast of RHOSLC, she is a well-established businesswoman with a massive net worth.

Barlow owns many businesses, including the marketing agency LUXE and the tequila brand VIDA Tequila. She is also the owner of Fresh Wolf, a men’s grooming business, and Tequila Ciudad.

She is a self-made millionaire. It is reported that her net worth is estimated to be $5 million.

Heather Gay

Gay is a businesswoman, author, and television personality from the United States. Gay was born on June 29, 1974, in Carmel, California, to John and Susan Deans. 

She was raised as a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Gay rose to prominence during the first season of the Bravo reality television series RHOSLC.

However, she has also appeared on other reality TV shows, like in the third season of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills: Ultimate Girls Trip, alongside co-star and cousin Rose. Gay has been on talk shows such as The Wendy Williams Show, Sherri, and Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen, in addition to The Real Housewives franchise. 

She appeared as a guest on Below Deck Adventure in late 2022. Talking about her personal life, Gay married Frank William “Billy” Gay III in July 2000 at the Laie Hawaii Temple. 

Billy is the grandson of notable business leader Frank William and the nephew of LDS Church general authority and former CEO of Huntsman Gay Global Capital (HGGC), Robert.

After 11 years of marriage, the couple divorced, however, the divorce was not finalized until 2014. To some extent, Gay’s net worth comes from the alimony she received.

However, she has her own business —  Beauty Lab + Laser. She and her business partner, Andrea Nord, launched the medical spa services firm in Murray, Utah, in 2017.

Gay has also written the New York Times Best Seller, Bad Mormon: A Memoir. She has an estimated net worth of $1.7 million.

Meredith Marks

Marks is a celebrity favorite and award-winning jewelry designer. Marks is a Chicago native who has always been interested in fashion. 

As an undergraduate, she studied visual arts, worked as a model, and then attended graduate school at Northwestern University, where she earned two diplomas —  her law degree, and an MM from the Kellogg Business School.

Marks became a wife and had children while working in real estate development and operating a health club. She was married to her husband, Seth, and has three children with him —  Brooks, Reid, and Chloe.

In 2020, Marks separated from her husband. When she was working in real estate, she discovered her calling in jewelry creation. 

She was lucky to be able to overcome these hurdles because she established her firm during the 2009 recession. Meredith started designing after studying at the GIA. 

Now, she is a successful businesswoman who has created jewelry for celebrities with the likes of Rihanna, Courteney Cox, Tina Fey, and Gwyneth Paltrow.

According to SCMP, she has a net worth of $1 million.

Whitney Rose

Whitney Rose was born in Utah, United States, on September 30, 1986. She grew up in a Mormon environment in her hometown. 

Steve Lesh, her father, is claimed to have suffered from addiction and substance misuse. Rose attended California’s St. Mary’s College, and she earned a Bachelor of Business Administration degree in 2016. 

Before getting her big break on RHOSLC, Rose worked at Nu Skin Enterprise. While working there, she met her present husband, Justine. 

He was her boss, yet their love knew no bounds. They began dating while still working at Nu Enterprises and continued even after Rose left the company.

They married shortly after learning that Rose was pregnant. They’ve been married for a time and have two children, Brooks and Bobbie.

Before appearing on RHOSLC, Rose was a well-established entrepreneur. She is well-known for her popular cosmetics business, Iris+Beau, an all-natural skincare brand, which was founded in 2017.

Recently, the Iris+Beau was renamed to Wild Rose Beauty. Most of Rose’s net worth comes from her company and RHOSLC.

It is reported that she has a net worth of $3 million.

Monica Garcia

Not much is known about Garcia since she is one of the new cast members of RHOSLC.

According to her Bravo profile, Garcia is Portuguese-Colombian and has her own baby goods company. She is also an excommunicated ex-Mormon.

On RHOSLC, Garcia is navigating her life as a busy mum of four while also dealing with her volatile relationship with her mother. The RHOSLC newcomer owns a baby goods company. 

Her love life looks to be tough right now since she is divorcing her spouse for the second time. She has also admitted to having an 18-month relationship with an unnamed individual in the supertease.

Her net worth has not been disclosed to date. However, it is estimated to be somewhere along the lines of $300,000 to $1 million.

Angie Katsanevas

Katsanevas is another new cast member who joined RHOSLC in the fourth season. Last season, she was defined as a business owner, wife, and mother by the reality network. 

According to Distractify, Katsanevas has been a hairdresser since the age of 21. She presently operates Lunatic Fringe salon, which has nine locations in Utah, Idaho, and Ohio. 

The salon provides a luxurious guest experience with multiple levels of talented stylists, according to its website. Katsanevas opened the first salon in Salt Lake City in 1999 with fellow stylist and husband Shawn Trujillo. 

Katsanevas is still together with her husband, the the two share a daughter, Elektra, together.

Angie’s net worth is unknown at the moment, however, the US Sun believes it to be approximately $5 million.
