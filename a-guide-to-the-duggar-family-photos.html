A Guide to Everyone in the Duggar Family

April 12, 2024
blog

In 2008, Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar invited TLC cameras into their home and introduced their sprawling brood to the world. Since 19 Kids and Counting (formerly, 17 Kids and Counting and 18 Kids and Counting) hit the small screen, viewers have seen the devout Christian family through courtships, marriages and new babies.

However, controversy struck in 2015 when Jim Bob and Michelle's eldest child, Josh Duggar, was accused of molesting five underage girls when he was a teenager, two of whom were his younger sisters.

19 Kids and Counting was swiftly canceled after Josh's molestation scandal and in 2022, he was sentenced to more than 12 years in federal prison on charges of receiving and possessing child pornography.

Now, eight years after going off air, the Duggar family is at the center of another show: Prime Video docuseries Shiny Happy People: Duggar Family Secrets. The docuseries promises to dive into the family's history and ties to the religious organization Institute in Basic Life Principles (IBLP) church.

Here's everything to know about the ever-growing reality TV family.

Jim Bob & Michelle Duggar

Married on July 21, 1984, the Duggar family matriarch, 56, and patriarch, 57, have welcomed 19 children over the course of their union.

The couple initially waited four years to have their first child, Josh. After conceiving again and having a miscarriage, which they say happened because Michelle was taking birth control and didn't know she was pregnant, they decided to let God "bless them with as many children as he saw fit."

With new babies arriving roughly every year and a half until 2009, they've worked hard to keep their marriage strong.

"We have a weekly date, even if it is just to take a walk in the park," Jim Bob, a former Arkansas politician, previously told PEOPLE. "We will go out to lunch or dinner and talk together about the kids, upcoming events, what we have coming up."

In 2020, the couple found a new passion: flipping houses in Arkansas.

Josh & Anna Duggar

Michelle and Jim Bob's eldest son Josh, 35, and his wife Anna, 34, wed in September 2008 and have seven children, whose names all start with the letter "M."

The couple share daughters Mackynzie, Meredith Grace, Maryella Hope and Madyson and sons Michael, Marcus and Mason. Meredith and Maryella were born during their father's molestation scandal, and the couple announced their pregnancy with their seventh and youngest child, Madyson, just a week before Josh was arrested for receiving and possessing child pornography. She was born that October while he awaited trial.

On April 29, 2021, U.S. Marshals arrested Josh and charged him with receiving and possessing child pornography some of which included photos and videos of children younger than 10. During a virtual court appearance in Arkansas the next day, he pleaded not guilty to the two charges against him.

Josh pleaded not guilty, but on Dec. 9, 2021, he was found guilty on both counts. Four months later, he was sentenced to 12 years and seven months in prison and was ordered to pay fines and special assessments of $50,100. He is currently serving his sentence in Seagoville, Texas.
