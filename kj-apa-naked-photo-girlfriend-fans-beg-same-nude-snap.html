RIVERDALE star KJ Apa shared nude photos of girlfriend Clara Berry on Instagram on Wednesday.

The Riverdale star captioned the shot: "There's nowhere else."

Back in June, fans slammed KJ after he claimed he didn't "need" to post about Black Lives Matter on social media.
