Scout Willis Topless In NYC — Her Shocking Instagram Anti-Nudity Protest

Rocking only a floral skirt and a super-confident attitude, the famous Hollywood daughter posted not one, but TWO topless pics on her personal Twitter account on May 28, using the hashtag #FreeTheNipple. The young actress/model is definitely not afraid to bare it all online — and on the streets — to show just how strongly she opposes Instagram’s nude photo ban!

“Legal in NYC but not on @instagram,” Scout captioned one topless photo of herself at an NYC flower shop. Scout also posted a pic of herself confidently strutting down an NYC street, alongside the caption: “What @instagram won’t let you see #FreeTheNipple”.

Don’t agree with Scout’s protest? She’s totally cool with that. “If you don’t like what you see, simply unfollow me,” Scout posted in a bold tweet.

We think she’s proven her point!

Instagram Has Censored Celebs Before

Instagram is famous for its super-strict rules that allow absolutely ZERO nudity on the photo-sharing site. Just last month, on April 29, the site threatened to shut down Rihanna‘s account after the singer posted ultra-sexy photos from a cover shoot for French magazine Lui.

Rihanna, 26, took the whole incident in stride. The Caribbean Queen fired back at Instagram’s censoring of her photos with a totally covered-up pic and the hilarious caption, ”Rih’s next magazine cover if it was up to Instagram.” RiRi’s photo scandal is just one example of an Instagram cover-up that Scout is SO not OK with!
