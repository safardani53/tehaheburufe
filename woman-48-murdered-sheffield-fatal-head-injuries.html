Woman, 48, found murdered in Sheffield house suffered fatal head injuries, cops say

October 06, 2024

A WOMAN found allegedly murdered in a house suffered fatal head injuries, according to cops.

Emily Sanderson, 48, was discovered in the empty property in the Hillsborough area of Sheffield.

Emily had been "reported missing" to cops on May 25 after not being "seen or heard from" for six days.

Her body was then tragically found at the property on Crofton Avenue earlier this week.

And a forensic post mortem has now confirmed that her cause of death was "head injuries".

Cops stressed that Emily's family is receiving support from specially trained officers and have asked for their privacy to be respected at this difficult time.
