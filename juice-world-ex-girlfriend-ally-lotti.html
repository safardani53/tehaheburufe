RISING rap star Juice Wrld died in December 2019 when he collapsed on a flight to Chicago.

The Lucid Dreams singer was 21 years old and in a relationship with Instagram influencer, Ally Lotti, at the time of his death.

Who is Juice Wrld's ex-girlfriend?

Ally Lotti is a famous Instagram influencer in the US and has 1.5million followers on the social media site.

Her following grew massively after it was revealed that she was dating Juice Wrld, whose real name was Jarad Anthony Higgins.

Lotti was born Alicia L Leon in Alabama. Her father is Black and her mother is Puerto Rican.

The 29-year-old net worth is estimated at around $700,000 as of 2021.

When did they start dating?

Lotti and Higgins started dating in 2018 before his untimely death in 2019.

The self-proclaimed influencer made their relationship public in November 2018 with a video post on Instagram of the two of them together.

The last time Lotti was with Higgins was on December 5, 2019, three days before his death.

Has she spoken about his death?

Lotti posted a photo on her Instagram story on December 2, 2021, in honor of Higgins birthday with the caption, "I want today B mentally much better. It's going. I'm every lost for words."

Mere days before Higgins' death in 2019, Lotti posted a birthday message on Twitter and said, "Before this day ends, I just wanna say happy birthday to my favorite person.

"This is your second birthday I spent with you and I'm so grateful.

"You deserve so much. I'm so proud of you and everything you’ve accomplished this past year.

"There is not a soul that could match your energy. I love you endlessly."

When was Ally Lotti arrested?

Lotti was arrested on January 14, 2023, on charges of drug possession and theft.

According to a jail inmate record obtained by XXL from the Crittenden County Sheriff’s Office in West Memphis, Arkansas: "Alicia L. Leon, was arrested on early Saturday morning (Jan. 14) on charges of possession of methamphetamine or cocaine with the purpose to deliver, possession of drug paraphernalia and theft of property."

After being jailed at the Crittenden County Detention center, she was released Saturday night after posting a $2,525 bond, HipHopDX reported.

Lotti was allegedly arrested alongside her reported boyfriend, Carter Jamison, who was also charged with theft of property but released without bond on Saturday morning.
