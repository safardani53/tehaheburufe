See Camille Grammer Act in New TV Gig!

October 09, 2024
blog

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills may have just wrapped its season, but Camille Grammer has already settled into a new TV role.

The reality star's guest appearance on CBS' $#*! My Dad Says airs Thursday night on the show's season finale, and UsMagazine.com has a sneak peek.

Though the role, admittedly, isn't much of a stretch.

As the above clips show, Camille, 42, appears as a divorced reality star who goes house hunting on her reality show. The character's name? Camille, of course.

Appearing opposite series stars Will Sasso and Nicole Sullivan in one scene, Camille offers a take on one of her most spoiled-sounding quotes from Real Housewives.

"I'm buying a house!" she enthuses on the episode. "It's 10,000 square feet, [but] we'll make due. The kids will share a wing. We're survivors." (On Housewives' Nov. 29 episode, Grammer griped that the 3,500 square foot home she shared with ex-husband Kelsey Grammer was "a little small for us.")

Grammer can add her latest TV gig to a growing list of industry credits, including small roles in Private Parts and an episode of her ex's hit show, Frasier.
