Get it, girl! Selena Gomez is far from the Disney princess we used to know. Now 22, the Wizards of Waverly Place alum shows off a decidedly more adult side in her latest video, “Good for You,” released online on Friday, June 26. In the clip, Gomez croons a stripped-down version of her hit featuring A$AP Rocky, and puts her a-maaaaazing bod on display.

“‘Cause I just wanna look good for you, good for you, uh-huh,” Gomez sings on a mellow beat. “I just wanna look good for you, good for you, uh-huh / Let me show you how proud I am to be yours / Leave this dress a mess on the floor / And still look good for you, good for you, uh-huh.”

In the three-minute clip directed by Sophie Muller which Gomez calls ‘beautiful and intimate,” the songbird writhes around on the floor as she sings to an unnamed beau in skimpy outfits including ripped denim, a silk dress, a wet white T-shirt, and in one steamy shower scene, nothing but her birthday suit.

Gomez’s sultry, sexy video is no doubt a part of her plan to silence the haters and live life on her own terms. “You can’t talk about anything that you want to share with people because it’s going to be ripped apart, and that’s what makes me really sad… I have a right to say what I’m going to say,” Gomez told New York City’s Z100 radio host Elvis Duran in a June 22 interview.
