BRIELLE Biermann showed off her side profile after getting painful double jaw surgery.

The Don't Be Tardy star, 24, had the procedure earlier this month to correct her overbite, which was caused by sucking her thumb until she was nine.

And it looked like Brielle was pleased she had the operation as she proudly showed off the results on her Instagram Stories on Saturday.

The Bravo star put her chin and jawline on full display by turning her face away from the camera while walking in the sun.

She captioned the short video: "Side profile looking better," with a smiling face emoji.

Brielle couldn't resist giving fans another glimpse at her new jaw again while driving in the car and filmed herself dancing at the camera with two male friends.

She shocked fans earlier this month by posting shocking post-surgery photos after her grueling double jaw surgery.

Posing in her hospital bed, the reality star could be seen with tubes around her face as well as ice packs.

The daughter of RHOA alum Kim Zolciak-Biermann showed off the bruising and swelling around her cheeks.

In her caption, the influencer wrote: "sooooo… i had double jaw surgery on august 23 to correct my tmj & 9 mm overjet overbite. this was NOT for aesthetics but for quality of life."

TMJ is Temporomandibular joint dysfunction, which causes jaw pain and restricted movement of the jaw.

GRUELING SURGERY

"I sucked my thumb til i was 9 ishhh which made the roof of my mouth suuuuuper narrow and my excessive overbite cause my teeth to have no contact whatsoever." she added.

Brielle admitted that her overbite stopped her from being able to bite into pizza and she would "almost choke" everytime she ate.

"i was so scared… i was completely dreading this surgery fearing the worst of the worst," she continued.

Brielle thanked her friends and family as well as the team of doctors who helped her get "thru the first two weeks, which are supposed to be the hardest."
