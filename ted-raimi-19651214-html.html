Theodore Raimi is an American actor, director, comedian and writer. He is known for his roles in the works of his brother Sam Raimi, including the fake Shemp in The Evil Dead, possessing Henrietta in Evil Dead II and Ted Hoffman in the Spider-Man trilogy. He later played the role of Henrietta in the Ash vs Evil Dead TV series, in which he also played the role of Chet Kaminski. He is also known for his roles as Lieutenant JG Tim O'Neill in seaQuest DSV and Joxer the Mighty in Xena: Warrior Princess and Hercules: The Legendary Journeys. Raimi has appeared with minor roles in a number of other movies and TV series, including Crimewave, Intruder, Shocker, Darkman, Candyman, Army of Darkness, Clear and Present Danger, The Grudge, Drag Me to Hell, and Oz. the Great. and Powerful, as well as the TV series ALF, Twin Peaks, Baywatch and Supernatural.
