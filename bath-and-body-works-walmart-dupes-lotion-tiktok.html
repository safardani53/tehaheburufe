TIKTOKERS are rejoicing after finding a $5 lotion dupe at Walmart that is supposed to be identical to items founds at Bath & Body Works.

The products are flying off the shelves as shoppers race to find the affordable alternative.

TikTok user @sararosaaa5 is a former Bath & Body Works employee who shared the dupes on her account, comparing them to other scents.

The coveted products found at Walmart are from the brand Scent Theory and retail at $4.98 each.

“There’s dupes for our products at Walmart for way cheaper,” said Sara.

The first Scent Theory product was the Wild Black Cherry body cream, which Sara says smells just like Black Cherry Merlot Ultimate Hydration Body Cream at Bath & Body Works.
